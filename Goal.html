Goal Statement

Mission Statement

The Dog was started in hopes of bringing happiness to as many communities through the Dog. Primarily, cards with the Dog graphic are handed out, as something out of the ordinary that may make give people a quick and unexpected laugh.

Vision Statement

All of the cards have been printed and handed out in hopes that others see it as a symbol of joy and levity. Beyond handing out cards, we strive to inspire kindness and hope in those who need it most.

Core Values


- Joy: Commitment to spreading happiness and positivity.
- Community: Dedication to engaging with and uplifting the local community.
- Creativity: Using imaginative characters and designs to bring smiles to people's faces.
- Compassion: Showing kindness and empathy to everyone in the community.
- Hope: Reminding people that, regardless of their current circumstances, there is something out there that they can find solace in.
- Lightheartedness: Show others that life can't be taken too seriously.

Call to Action

If you hope to join the cause, here are some things you can do: - Share photos of "dog" cards found around the community.
- Volunteer to distribute cards and spread joy.
- Donate to support the creation of new characters and initiatives.
- Share the Dog image on social media.
- Inspire others to join the campaign.
